Registree revolutionizes university data

By building a privacy-preserving system for data ownership using the Ethereum blockchain and distributed databases.

Ownership of your data implies exclusive rights, i.e. the right to exclude others from using your data. Today, students have no control over what happens with their data.

At Registree, we believe that you should own your data and have full control how it is used! To realize this vision, we built a platform where universities can store sensitive student data in a shared database that guarantees privacy and gives students full control over who has access to their sensitive data.

Universities want to provide the optimal support for each and every student. With our Registree dashboard we give them the perfect tool to do so. Students want to find the right employer and employers the perfect graduate. Our platform connects both sides and overcomes market frictions.

Students

Blockchain powered decentralized student database

Registree uses a combination of the latest blockchain and distributed database technology to securely store student data. Registree does not have data access, students own their data. Universities are the only creators of the data which guarantees that data obtained from Registree is always 100% accurate.

Universities

Data service provider for universities

Registree provides revolutionary data services for universities, completely free of charge. These services provide a more holistic and uniquely detailed picture of student progress. Registree gives universities the right tools to provide the perfect support system for every student and help them achieve their goals. An early warning system can help lecturers to identify students who might be struggling so that they can make sure no student falls through the cracks.

Employers

Graduate Portal for Employers

Registree overcomes costly search frictions in labor markets and reduces employers' cost of finding graduates. Graduates are able to find more employment options. Through our collaboration with universities we are able to provide access to the universe of graduates, not only a small subset like jobs portals.

Why Registree?

Graduates, Employers, and Universities all struggle with insufficient access to data.

There is no system at present that addresses all three groups issues

Graduates

Strict Privacy laws make legal access to student data very difficult

Despite this, students have no control over their sensitive private data (eg. Cambridge Analytica)

Universities

It is difficult to get a holistic view of students. Impossible to compare student performance after transferring from another university.

Verifying certificates is costly and time consuming

Employers

Extremely costly search for qualified graduates across multiple platforms.

Insufficient data available about graduates. As a consequence, large information asymmetries.

How will recruiters be able to query students' data?

Recruiters will set specific search criteria and send a notification of their interest to the student. Recruiters will not see which students are notified rather they will be told that their are students who fulfill the specified criteria. Once a student receives this notification she can accept or reject it. Only if the student accepts the notification will her identity be shared with the recruiter.

How does registree protect against algorithmic and recruiter bias?

Registree's algorithms are based off the search criteria stipulated by recruiters. Registree will not provide the functionality for recruiters to search for candidates using prejudicial search criteria.

Who can contact students?

Registree will stictly vet all third parties allowed onto the platform. Our objective is to provide students with access to high-quality bona fide opportunities. Only recruiters who fulfill these criteria will be allowed to contact students.

What happens if a student has a job and does not wish to be contacted?

From the student's registree dashboard, she will be able to easily toggle whether she wishes to receive notifications from recruiters or not. Once a student has disabled this functionality, recruiters will not be able to send notifications to this student.
